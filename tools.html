Farming Tools

Plow

A plow is a tool used in farming for initial cultivation of soil in preparation for sowing seed or planting.

Tractor

A tractor is a powerful motor vehicle with large rear wheels, used chiefly on farms for hauling equipment and trailers.

Harvester

A harvester is a machine for harvesting crops, such as grains or cotton.

Agricultural Drones

Agricultural Drones are unmanned aerial vehicles(UAVs)are equipped with sensors and cameras that can monitor crop health,map fields,and spray pesticides

Soil sensors

These sensors measure soil moisture,temperature,and nutrients to help farmers optimize irrigation and fertilization
